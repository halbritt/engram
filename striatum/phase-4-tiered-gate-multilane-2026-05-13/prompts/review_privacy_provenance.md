# Review Phase 4 Privacy And Provenance Evidence

Review the fresh Tier 0-2 evidence for local-first privacy and Striatum
provenance.

Focus on:

- no committed raw corpus text, belief values, entity names, conversation
  titles, prompts, completions, or private paths;
- aggregate-only evidence and redacted ids;
- no cloud dependency or user-data egress;
- branch consistency, real process executions, and truthful bylines;
- no reuse of RFC 0032-quarantined artifacts as gate evidence;
- whether the workflow clearly blocks full-corpus Phase 4 until accepted.

Lead with findings ordered by severity. Use `accept_with_findings` if the
evidence is privacy-safe but promotion remains blocked. Use `needs_revision`
for private data exposure, provenance mismatch, or overclaimed authorization.

Write `docs/operations/phase4-build/tiered-gate-multilane-2026-05-13/REVIEW_privacy_provenance.md`
with the exact lowercase `author:` line from the work packet.
