# Ergonomics Design Review For RFC 0038

Review the implemented UI as an operator ergonomics and decision-cost pass.
This is in addition to the normal security/correctness/operator-contract
review cycle.

Focus on:

- information density and scan order;
- whether the next correct decision is easy to reach;
- keyboard flow and focus behavior;
- responsive behavior on narrow screens;
- warning comprehension and truthful status language;
- whether future/backlog states are visually distinct from implemented states;
- whether the design avoids marketing layout, decorative gradients/orbs, and
  vague product copy.

Do not edit implementation files. Publish the required finding artifact and
record a Striatum verdict.
