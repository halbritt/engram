| D048 | accepted | `beliefs.valid_from` / `valid_to` represent fact validity only; row lifecycle uses `status`, `superseded_by`, and a new `closed_at` column. | P022/P024 review found `valid_to = now()` on close fragmented historical fact intervals and made temporal contradiction auto-resolution unreachable on real corpus evidence.
