Interpretation

  • qwen_35b_a3b_iq4_xs_d034 failed the hard provenance gate with five unordered-message-id failures.
  • qwen_27b_q5_k_m_d034 had the best Pk and WindowDiff, perfect schema/provenance validity, and much better boundary F1 than Qwen 35B, but it was very slow.
  • gemma_26b_a4b_q4_k_m_d034 had the strongest raw boundary F1 and good throughput, but fragmented much more aggressively.
  • Raw SuperDialseg boundary F1 alone should not drive model selection.
